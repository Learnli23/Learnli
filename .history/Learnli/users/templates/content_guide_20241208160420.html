Introduction

Books uploaded to the LearnLi platform must adhere to strict quality and ethical standards to preserve LearnLi’s mission of being an academic hub for learners, educators, and institutions...

Guiding Principles for Uploaded Books Moderation

  • Educational Merit: All uploaded books must align with LearnLi’s goal of providing accurate and valuable educational resources.
  • Ethical Compliance: Books must be free from harmful or discriminatory content.
  • Legal Adherence: All content must comply with copyright, intellectual property, and data privacy regulations.
  • Inclusivity: Ensure content supports diversity and avoids offensive biases.
  • Content Integrity: Books should maintain factual accuracy and avoid misleading claims.

Flaggable and Inappropriate Content for Uploaded Books

Categories of Flaggable Content

  • Offensive or Harmful Material: Hate speech, offensive slurs, or discriminatory remarks...
  • Misinformation and Academic Inaccuracy: False or misleading claims...

Degrees of Inappropriateness for Uploaded Books

Low Degree: Typos, formatting issues, or minor factual errors.
Action: Approve flagged content with recommendations for improvement.

Moderate Degree: Misleading or incomplete information that could confuse readers.
Action: Escalate to a reviewer for further assessment.

High Degree: Content promoting harm, illegal activities, or blatant misinformation.
Action: Reject and immediately remove from the platform.

Procedures for Moderation Actions

Approval of Flagged Books

  1. Mark the book as approved in the system.
  2. Notify the flagger and the uploader with a brief explanation.
