Guiding Principles for Lesson Moderation

  • Educational Alignment: Lessons must reflect educational standards relevant to the intended audience and subject matter.
  • Legal Compliance: Lessons must adhere to copyright, intellectual property, and data privacy laws.
  • Content Accuracy: Lessons should provide accurate, well-researched, and verifiable information.
  • Inclusivity and Respect: Lessons must be inclusive and free from harmful or discriminatory content.
  • Engagement and Clarity: Content must be clear, well-organized, and engaging.
