Introduction

Blog posts are a vital aspect of LearnLi, enabling users to express ideas, share insights, and engage in meaningful discussions...

Guiding Principles

  • Free Expression: Encourage creative and academic freedom while maintaining respect and adherence to standards.
  • Content Integrity: Ensure blog posts are accurate, truthful, and aligned with LearnLi's objectives.
  • Inclusivity: Avoid bias, discrimination, or harm while promoting inclusivity.
  • Transparency: Ensure moderation decisions are consistent and clearly communicated.
  • Escalation in Ambiguities: Escalate complex cases for further review or expertise.

Flaggable and Inappropriate Blog Content

Categories of Flaggable Content

  • Harmful or Offensive Content: Hate speech, harassment, graphic violence, etc.
  • Academic Violations: Plagiarism, uncredited use of intellectual property.
  • Misinformation: Posts spreading false or unverifiable claims.
  • Spam or Malicious Content: Irrelevant promotional content or phishing links.
  • Non-Compliant Content: Privacy law violations or breaches of copyright.

Degrees of Inappropriateness

Low Degree (Minor Issues): Typos or slight inaccuracies.
Action: Approve but recommend edits.

Moderate Degree (Borderline Inappropriate): Questionable opinions or partially accurate information.
Action: Escalate for further review.

High Degree (Severe Violations): Explicitly harmful or illegal content.
Action: Reject and remove immediately.
