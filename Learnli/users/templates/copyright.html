Learnli Policies

1. Terms of Service (ToS)

These Terms of Service ("ToS") govern the use of the LearnLi platform. By registering or using LearnLi, users agree to abide by these terms, which are subject to periodic updates.

User Responsibilities

  • Account Security: Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials. Sharing login information is prohibited.
  • Content Compliance: All content uploaded must be original or appropriately licensed. Users must ensure their content complies with applicable intellectual property and other relevant laws.
  • Behavior: Users must engage in respectful, non-discriminatory interactions and comply with platform guidelines for communication.

Payment Terms and Subscription Details

  • Subscriptions are available in 1-month, 3-month, 6-month, or 12-month periods, with different rates based on user status.
  • LearnLi deducts a 10% commission on course and eBook sales, with the remaining revenue distributed to content creators after payment processing fees.
  • Refunds are available under specific conditions, such as faulty content or billing errors. Subscription-based services are non-refundable after usage.

Prohibited Activities

  • Sharing or distributing account credentials.
  • Uploading content not owned or licensed by the user.
  • Engaging in spamming, system overloads, or other malicious activities.

Dispute Resolution

  • Mediation: Users should first attempt to resolve disputes through mediation before seeking legal action.
  • Legal Jurisdiction: Any unresolved disputes will be settled under the jurisdiction defined in the governing law section.

2. Privacy Policy

LearnLi is committed to safeguarding user privacy and ensuring compliance with data protection laws such as GDPR, CCPA, and others.

User Consent

  • By using LearnLi, users consent to the collection, use, and sharing of their personal data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
  • Users can withdraw their consent at any time through account settings or by contacting support.

Data Collection, Use, and Protection

  • LearnLi collects data such as user identity (name, email), financial data for transactions, and activity logs to improve user experience.
  • Data is protected through encryption both in transit and at rest.
  • User data is shared only with trusted third-party providers (e.g., payment processors) necessary to perform services.

User Rights

  • Access and Portability: Users can request access to their personal data and obtain a copy.
  • Data Deletion: Users have the right to request deletion of their data unless retention is required for legal obligations.

3. Anti-Plagiarism Policy

LearnLi upholds the integrity of academic content on the platform by ensuring all uploaded material is original or properly licensed.

Original Content Standards

  • All uploaded content must either be created by the user or licensed for use on LearnLi.
  • Proper citations must be provided for any third-party materials incorporated into course content.

Plagiarism Detection and Handling

  • LearnLi uses plagiarism detection software to verify the originality of submitted content.
  • Content found to be plagiarized will be removed, and users may face account suspension or termination based on severity.
